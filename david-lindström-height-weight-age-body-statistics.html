David Lindström is a Swedish actor and musician who is best known for having been cast as Sigurd Snake-in-the-Eye in Vikings as well as for playing the role of Simon Nilsson in Blue Eyes. In addition to this, his other on-screen roles include Manne in Tsunami, Olle in The Yearning Room, Andreas Hjelm in Beck, and Kenny Kylberg in Maria Wern.

    David Lindström Facts

  • From 2014 to 2015, he brought to life the character of Simon Nilsson in the political drama television series, Blue Eyes, which is focused on 2 female leads against the backdrop of the rise of a far-right political party.
  • He has played the character named Sigurd Snake-in-the-Eye, Ragnar and Aslaug’s 3rd son who is killed by his brother Ivar after years of bullying, in the historical action-adventure drama television series, Vikings. David Lindström co-starred in the show alongside the likes of Travis Fimmel, Katheryn Winnick, Clive Standen, Gustaf Skarsgård, Gabriel Byrne, Linus Roache, Alexander Ludwig, Ben Robson, Jasper Pääkkönen, Patrick Smith, Marco Ilsø, and Alex Høgh Andersen.
