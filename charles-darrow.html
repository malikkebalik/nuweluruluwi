Biography

Main developer and claimed inventor of the classic board game Monopoly who invented some of the iconic drawings such as the red GO arrow, the railroad locomotives, the water works faucet, the electric company light bulb, and chance question marks. He first pitched the game to Milton Bradley before The Parker Brothers eventually bought the rights. During the Stock Market Crash of 1929 he lost his job at a sales company.
