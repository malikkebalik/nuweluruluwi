Swae Lee Expecting First Child With Girlfriend Victoria Kristine

Rae Sremmurd’s Swae Lee is expecting his first child with his girlfriend Victoria Kristine.

The news was revealed by Kristine on her Instagram page on Tuesday (August 30) with photos from the baby shower. The shower had a safari theme with “Baby Brown” displayed throughout their home, with the Mississippi rapper — real name Khalif Brown — appearing in various photos.

“Our little wild one is on the way,” she wrote in the caption.

Kristine also confirmed they’re expecting a baby boy by writing “SON OTW @swaelee” on her Intagram Stories, before showing off a new Land Rover gifted by her “baby daddy” for the special occasion.

Victoria Kristine first announced her pregnancy on Instagram earlier in August, writing: “Couldn’t of dreamt up anything more special than you my baby.”

Swae Lee spoke to Office Magazine in 2020 about his upcoming projects and hopes for the future, mentioning his own potential children.

“I’m working on my album, and I’m working on [SREMM4LIFE],” he said at the time. “And I’m working on Human Nature, my debut album as a solo artist. For 2021, I hope we can all just get along. That everybody can get along, that I can get this album out, and that we can go back outside, and that people can just see people for who they are, the good in people.”
