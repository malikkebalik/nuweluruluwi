Ciara's husband Russell Wilson lists the reasons why she is his greatest love in the sweetest way

Ciara and her husband Russell Wilson are feeling the love this Valentine's Day, sharing adorable tributes to one another on social media. While Ciara released a new song about Russell being her "Greatest Love," he also released his own video about why he knows for sure that his wife is also his greatest love.

In Ciara's new song, she sings about her husband and their relationship, expressing just how lucky she is to be his wife. The lyrics read, "I should’ve known when you took my son as your own / I ain’t saying I ain’t like all the carats and the stone / It’s just your love that I want / Now it’s you and me against, ‘gainst the entire world, the entire world / I see our love when I look at our baby girl, I’m a lucky girl.”

Her new song came with a lovely music video that showed the singer performing in an array of beautiful outfits. It is her third single off of her upcoming album, following "Dose" and "Level Up."

Supporting his wife and her newly released single, Russel made his own video, sharing his own reasons why Ciara is his greatest love. Before anything else, he said that she makes the world a better place and inspires him to do good. He then goes on to say how wonderful she is as a mother, doing anything and everything for their two kids. Lastly, it's that she makes him a better man. Wilson says that his wife strengthens her faith, and it's what makes their love work.

Ciara made sure to let her husband know how happy he's made her, saying that his video made her face hurt from smiling. She finished her short message by saying "You always make me speechless. #Blessed"

A SMITTEN COUPLE

Ciara and Russell have been showing their fans just why they deserve the moniker "relationship goals." Aside from their sweet "Greatest Love" tributes to one another, the way they act on a daily basis has proven that their love for one another knows no bounds.

Recently, Russell took a quick video of Ciara heading down from their staircase, saying that his lady was "coming down the way" for their date night. Absolutely taken aback by his wife's beauty, his caption wrote: "can't believe my eyes... love you, Mrs. Wilson."

Fans of the couple are happy that they seem to be falling more and more in love with one another every day and have been sharing their good wishes for the couple. In the post's comment section, their fans wrote:

"@cecesupreme_: Happiness shows you are lucky man and she’s a lucky women for you both to have each other. Black Love"

"@mzatlshaybutta: I love the love between you guys!"

@beenie_beans: Damn.... she's stunning. You're a lucky man. Treat her good"

Ciara and Russell have been married since 2016, and the two share a daughter named Sienna Princess Wilson. She also has a son from a previous relationship with Future, whom they named Future Jr.
