Capital City Public Market in downtown Boise will open season in Garden City

BOISE, Idaho (CBS2) — Capital City Public Market is set to open this season next week.

But its location will kick off the season in a different spot.

Organizers say the Capital City Public Market will open April 10 by the Boise River at 304 E. 34th Street in Garden City and "will run there until we are allowed to return to our home in the heart of downtown Boise.

Operation hours will be from 10 a.m. until 2 p.m. every Saturday.

The Market is expecting between 40-50 vendors to start the season, but will likely add more as additional space becomes available.
