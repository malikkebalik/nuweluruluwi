Taylor Hill Bio (Wiki)

Who is Taylor Hill?

Taylor Hill is an American model and an actress. She has blown up every stage from magazine covers to runway, exotic locations around the world, and on the internet through her poses, confidence, and unique photogenity.

Taylor Hill: Birth Facts, Early Life

Taylor Hill was born on March 5, 1996, in Palatine, Illinois, US. Her full name is Taylor Marie Hill.

Her father is David Glenn Hill and her mother is Jenifer Rea. Likewise, she was raised at a barn in Granby, Colorado along with her sisters Logan Rae and Mackinley, and a brother Chase. Mackinley and Chase are also models like her.

Furthermore, Hill is of mixed caucasian descent and American nationality.

As per academics, Taylor Hill graduated from Pomona High School in Arvada, Colorado.

Taylor Hill: Career, Professional Life

Taylor Hill was discovered by talent agent Jim Jordan in 2011 at the age of 14 at a ranch in Granby, Colorado. She was given contracts with a number of modeling agencies. In 2013, she started her modeling career after featuring in Intimissimi’s catalog.

Now only at the age of 26, she is the next big thing in the online and modeling world. She has graced many covers of editorials like American, British, French, Spanish, Japanese, Arabian, Mexican, and Turkish Vogue, Harper’s Bazaar, French, Dutch and Australian Elle, W, Madame Figaro, Dazed, Love, V, Russian Número, Pop Magazine, British, Spanish, Turkish, Italian Cosmopolitan, Russh Magazine, Grazia, Glamour, and Italian Marie Claire.

Furthermore, Taylor Hill has worked with the likes of Versace, Valentino, Chanel, Armani, Moschino, Dolce & Gabbana, Brandon Maxwell, Balmain, Givenchy, Tommy Hilfiger, Fendi, H&M, Ralph Lauren, Kenzo, Alexander Wang, Lanvin, DKNY, Marc Jacobs, Miu Miu, Michael Kors and Bottega Veneta.

Awards, Nominations

In August 2015, she won “Model of the Year” on social media at Fashion Media Awards. In 2016, she was listed No. 17 on Forbes’ “The World’s Top-Earning Models” list, with estimated earnings of $4 million.
