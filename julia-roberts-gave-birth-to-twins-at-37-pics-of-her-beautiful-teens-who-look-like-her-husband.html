Julia Roberts Gave Birth to Twins at 37 Pics of Her 'Beautiful' Teens Who Look like Her Husband

  • Julia Roberts gave birth to twins at 37.
  • The mom of three admitted that her kids look like her husband, Danny Moder.
  • Moder often shares their kids' pictures online, where fans call them beautiful.

Julia Roberts and her husband, Danny Moder, have three kids. Their oldest kids, Phinnaeus and Hazel, 18, are twins. The couple's youngest child is Henry, 16.

Roberts is proud of her marriage and the happiness she and Moder have found together. The two strive to keep their marriage private and their kids away from the limelight.

Roberts gave birth to Phinneaus and Hazel in November 2004 at a Los Angeles hospital. The twins were born healthy.

Danny Moder and Julia Roberts at the Gala in the Garden sponsored by Bottega Veneta in Los Angeles on October 10, 2015 | Source: Getty Images

The actress gave birth to Phinnaeus and Hazel at 37. A source said the twins arrived at around 3 a.m. on Sunday, November 28, 2004. Henry was born on June 18, 2007. On becoming a mother in her late 30s, Roberts said her kids chose her to be her steward and shepherd in life when she was ready.

The mom of three fears at times that she will mess up as a mother, but she believes her kids came into her life at a moment when she was ready to be their mother. When her kids were young, the actress made sure to have fun with them, scare them, and even laugh at them.

Danny Moder, Julia Roberts, Kelly Slater, Phinnaeus, Henry Daniel, and Hazel Moder at a celebration of the launch of Outerknown in Malibu, California, on August 29, 2015 | Source: Getty Images

She once hilariously revealed that Henry thought that when people were talking about the twins in their family, they were referring to him and his siblings instead of just Phinnaeus and Hazel.

Roberts does not only have fun with her kids, but she does her best to protect them from the harms of fame. When the kids were younger, they knew that their mom was famous but never understood the true sense of that. Her kids would mostly see tabloid headlines when they were in stores, and Roberts found such moments uncomfortable.

The tabloid headlines, which weren't true, still hurt Roberts' feelings, especially when they speculated that her marriage was ending when she was happily married and raising her kids. Due to these challenges, Roberts and Moder wanted to raise their kids out of the public eye, so they moved from Los Angeles.

The actress and Moder have a ranch in New Mexico, but they used to stay in Los Angeles, where they worked. However, as per a friend, the two didn't want their kids to grow up in the show business culture of Los Angeles. The couple later moved to San Fransisco for their kids. There, the kids are not viewed as movie star kids, and no one cares who their mother is.

Moder also posts photos of their three kids online, and most of his pictures receive positive fan reactions.

As a protective mom, the "Ocean's Eleven" star revealed in 2019 that she has banned her kids from using social media to limit their exposure to the pressures of the modern world.

The actress limited what they watch on TV and introduced family meetings. She took these steps because it's so easy these days for kids to have the world in their hands—its pressures and resources—and misuse it. Roberts believes that as a parent, it's her job to keep her kids off social media because they don't need that at the moment.

Aside from protecting her kids, Roberts makes sure to give them unconditional love so that they can handle challenges in today's world. She also helps her kids navigate the issues of today.

When Hazel was going through a hard time and had lost hope, her mom took that opportunity to make her feel that she still had a voice. She took Hazel to her first Women's March in Washington to show her that she had a place in this world where she could still uphold what she believed in.

Danny Moder and Hazel Moder arrive at the premiere of "Flag Day" during the 74th Cannes Film Festival in Cannes, France, on July 10, 2021. | Source: Getty Images

According to the 56-year-old mother, Hazel is "one of a kind." When the teen attended the 74th Cannes Film Festival in 2021 with her dad, she opted for a simple look and even shrugged off her mother's suggestion to wear eyeliner. Hazel was just happy to participate in the event with her father.

Danny Moder and Hazel Moder arrive at the premiere of "Flag Day" during the 74th Cannes Film Festival in Cannes, France, on July 10, 2021. | Source: Getty Images

However, Hazel also has a penchant for her mom's prized possessions. Roberts revealed that her daughter once went through her closet looking for a prom dress, and she tried out the actress' black and white Valentino gown— the one she wore when she won an Academy Award in 2001. Hazel found the dress to be too big for her, but Roberts remembers how stunning her daughter looked in it.

Julia Roberts at the 73rd Annual Academy Awards in Los Angeles, California, on March 25, 2001. | Source: Getty Images

Roberts has done a lot to love, protect, and be there for her kids. Still, she says there is no mastering mothering. As per the actress, the best way to navigate motherhood is to know that as a mom, you cannot do everything, so you should not feel ashamed or fearful about asking for help.

Julia Roberts at the 73rd Annual Academy Awards in Los Angeles, California, on March 25, 2001. | Source: Getty Images

The "Pretty Woman" star admitted in 2022 that she felt lightheaded when her twins were about to go to college. She was also fascinated and excited for them because she never went to college, and her kids were going to experience it.

Roberts always thinks that her kids look like her, but when her husband comes home from work, and she sees him, it dawns on her that her children look like Moder. The dad of three sometimes shares their kids' photos online, which receive many comments from their fans.
