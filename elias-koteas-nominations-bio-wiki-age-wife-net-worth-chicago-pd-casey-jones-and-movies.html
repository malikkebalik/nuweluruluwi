Elias Koteas Actor

Koteas has played major roles in Some Kind of Wonderful (1987), The Adjuster (1991), Exotica (1994), Crash (1996), Fallen (1998), and Then Tjin Red Line (1998) among other films.

He is also known for playing the lead role of Thomas Daggett in the American film The Prophecy (1995) and portraying sports-crazed vigilante Casey Jones in the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles films.

On Television, he appeared in The Sopranos Season Four as Dominic Palladino and in Season Two finale of House as a man who shoots Dr. Gregory House. From 2014 to 2018, he starred on the NBC Chicago Fire spin-off Chicago P.D.

Then he played Alvin “Al” Olinsky, a longtime undercover detective in the Intelligence Unit. At the end of the 2018 season, His character was knifed in prison and died in surgery.
