Biography

Judy Trammell was born in Dallas, TX on May 29, 1958. Dallas Cowboy Cheerleader in the 1980s and head choreographer for the team, she is known for being on Country Music Television’s reality television show Dallas Cowboys Cheerleaders: Making the Team. She also choreographed for various music videos, television shows, beauty pageants, and music awards. Reality star Melissa Rycroft also known from The Bachelor and Dancing With The Stars, joined her as one of the Dallas Cowboys Cheerleaders for seasons 1 and 2 of Dallas Cowboys Cheerleaders: Making the Team. She attended R.L. Turner High School in Dallas, where she trained in jazz, pom, drill team, and tap dance. She became a Dallas Cowboys cheerleader in 1980 and was a show group member and group leader through 1984. In 1984 she was made assistant choreographer. She was made head choreographer in 1991.
