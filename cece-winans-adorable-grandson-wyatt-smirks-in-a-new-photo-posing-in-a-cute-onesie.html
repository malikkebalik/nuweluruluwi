CeCe Winans' Adorable Grandson Wyatt Smirks in a New Photo Posing in a Cute Onesie

Gospel artist Cece Winans' daughter, Ashley Love, shared the most adorable photo of her son showing off his cute smirk as he posed for the camera.

On Wednesday, Cece Winans' daughter, Ashley Love, shared the cutest picture of her five-week-old baby, Wyatt Thomas Philips, on her Instagram page.

The cute baby smiled as he posed for the camera in the adorable snapshot. Baby Wyatt stood upright, supported possibly by his mother's hands which held him in place.

CeCe Winans visiting Build Series in New York City in March 2017. | Photo: Getty Images

Wyatt was clad in a lovely white onesie which covered his hands and feet. Given the smile on his face, the little tot seemed to enjoy the attention he was getting. His mother noted the smirk in the caption to her post, which read,

"It's the smirk for me."

Many of Ashley's fans and friends commented on her post, including Yvette Nicole Brown, who gushed over baby Wyatt's sweet face. One fan said Wyatt is precious while another fan described him as adorable.

Ashley's latest post sees her continue with the trend of sharing updates about her baby before and after his birth. In October, she shared a picture from her baby shower that showed her posing with her mom and singer Amy Grant.

When asked what the secret to their union was, she said it has to do with having God in their lives.

In the photo, Ashley posed between her mother and Grant, who placed their hands on her baby bump. In the caption to the post, she thanked both women for throwing her the best baby shower.

On December 28, the proud mom shared an adorable photo of the newborn baby Wyatt sleeping peacefully while clad in a gray onesie and baby cap. In her caption to the post, she introduced the worldto her baby, with the words:

"World, meet Wyatt Thomas Phillips. He is the absolute best... Our lives are forever changed in the best way possible..."

Ashley shares Wyatt with her husband, Kenny Phillips, who she has previously described as her perfect man. The couple tied the knot in September 2017.

Ashley is Winans and her husband, Alvin Love's only daughter. The couple also shares a son, Alvin Love III, who produced and co-wrote her mom's 2017 album, "Let Them Fall in Love," which earned her two Grammy Award wins.

Love and Winans got married in June 1984 and will celebrate their 37th wedding anniversary this year; a feat for a Hollywood marriage.

When asked what the secret to their union is during a 1998 interview with Ebony, she said it has to do with having God in their lives and as the center of their marriage, while Love added that they communicate well.

With a loving husband and great marriage, two great kids, a cute grandson, and an excellent career, one can say Winans has everything to be thankful to God for, and she will be the first to admit that.
