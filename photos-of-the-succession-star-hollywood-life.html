Sarah Snook is an Australian actress best known for playing Shiv Roy in the hit HBO series ‘Succession,’ which ran for 4 seasons and ended in May 2023. She’s been nominated for two Emmys for her astounding performance in the show, and is nominated for a 2024 Golden Globe.

Sarah starred alongside Brian Cox, Matthew Macfadyen, Jeremy Strong, Kieran Culkin, Alan Ruck, and more in the critically acclaimed series created by Jesse Armstrong. The show has won the Golden Globe for Best Television Series — Drama twice so far. In 2023, Sarah confirmed she had given birth to her baby as she bid farewell to the show.
