TOMORROW X TOGETHER WORLD TOUR ACT SWEET MIRAGE FINALE Dates Set For December

TXT has once again proven its ticket-selling prowess by completely selling out their “TOMORROW X TOGETHER WORLD TOUR ‘ACT: SWEET MIRAGE’ FINALE” on November 3. This highly anticipated encore performance will fascinate fans live on December 2 and 3 at Seoul’s Gocheok Sky Dome, with additional internet broadcasting accessible via the worldwide fandom network Weverse.

They started their concert tour in March at the KSPO DOME in Seoul, and since then they have visited 15 different countries and performed 27 thrilling concerts. Their world tour has taken them to fascinating places like Singapore, Taipei, important Japanese cities like Osaka and Saitama, six different locations in the United States, Jakarta, and Bulacan.

The tour is noteworthy because it included Tomorrow X Together’s first-ever performances in a North American stadium and a Japanese dome, showcasing the band’s meteoric rise with a scale that is more than double that of their previous outing, ACT: LOVE SICK.

Concurrent with the excursion, the band’s third studio album, “Chapter of Names: FREEFALL,” catapulted them to new heights by earning the “double million seller” designation in its first week of release. They also broke another record by having nine albums debut at the top of Japan’s Oricon Weekly Album Rankings, making them the most successful foreign act of all time in that country.
