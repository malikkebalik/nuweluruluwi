Wes Ramsey- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

Wesley Albert Ramsey was born on October 6, 1977, in Louisville, Kentucky, United States. His father, William Ramsey, an orthopedic surgeon, and mother, Linda Ramsey, a painter and sculptor, raised him. He was raised alongside his two brothers, William and Warren Ramsey.

Ramsey has a Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) from New York City’s famed Juilliard School since 2000. He was a part of Group 29 (1996-2000) at Juilliard School, alongside Morena Baccarin and Glenn Howerton.

Wes Ramsey- Professional Career

Wes Ramsey’s career began on the American television soap opera Guiding Light, where he played Sam Spencer from 2000 to 2008. Later, in 2009, he co-starred with Connor Weil and Molly Burnett in 19 episodes of Days of Our Lives as Owen Kent. He has also appeared in a number of television shows, including Venice: The Series, Grey’s Anatomy, and many others.

Ramsey first appeared in the American drama film Way Off-Broadway in 2001, where he acted under the direction of Dan Kay. In addition, in 2005, he played Martin Breedlove in the independent film Slippery Slope. His other film credits include Brotherhood of Blood, Dracula’s Guest, and others. He also appeared in plays such as As Much As You Can and Grace Notes and Anvils.
