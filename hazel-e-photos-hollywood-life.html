Hazel-E Photos Hollywood Life

Former ‘Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood’ star Hazel-E is a publicist, brand consultant, entrepreneur, and rapper. Hazel-E was one of the original cast members of ‘Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood.’ She is originally from Cincinnati, Ohio, and was raised in Houston, Texas. Hazel-E got engaged to model De’Von Waller in Oct. 2019 after meeting each other through social media when he messaged her on Instagram. She previously dating Rose Burgandy, Katt Williams, and Yung Berg. Hazel-E and De’Von welcomed their newborn baby girl named Ava in June 2020. The couple starred on the summer edition of ‘Marriage Boot Camp: Hip Hop Edition’ in 2020 to test their relationship and see if De’Von was only in it for the clout, according to Hazel-E.
