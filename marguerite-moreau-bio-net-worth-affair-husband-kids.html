Early Life

Marguerite C. Moreau was born on the 25th of April, 1977, to parents Anne L.(Viafora) and Stephen Charles Moreau. Born and raised in Riverside, California, not much is known about Moreau’s early life. Details on her siblings have never been revealed to the media, and it is assumed that Moreau is the lone child. For her education, Moreau attended Vassar College and graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Political Science in 1999.

Professional Career

Moreau was hooked on acting from the young age of 14 and started out as a child actress. Her first TV appearance came in the television series Wonder Years. A few years later, Moreau also starred in the movie Mighty Ducks and played the part of a young Connie Moreau. Following her graduation, Moreau made an appearance in the 2001 comedy flick, Wet Hot American Summer. Her role as Katie pushed Moreau towards worldwide acclaim and she reprised the role in the sequel movies Wet Hot American Summer: First Day of Camp (2015) and the sequel Wet Hot American Summer: Ten Years Later (2017).

Moreau’s next blockbuster appearance came in 2002 when she was cast on Queen of the Damned. Based on the books by Ann Rice, the movie pushed Moreau to land the leading role in the TV miniseries Firestarter: Rekindled. Since then, Moreau’s career has been on a steady rise and the star has barely looked back.

Over the years, Moreau has been cast in a number of movies including, Two Days (2003), Easy (2003), Runaway Jury (2003), Off the Lip (2004), Ingineous (2009), Wake (2009), Hip (2010), and most recently on Paddleton (2019), and Into the Ashes (2019). Moreau’s accolades in TV series are not too shabby either. Over the years, she has been seen in The Secret World of Alex Mack (1994-1998), Amazing Grace (1995), Killer Instinct (2005), Lost (2005), Shameless (2011-2013), Grey’s Anatomy (2013), The People v. O.J. Simpson: American Crime Story (2016), A Night to Regret (2018), and most recently on Tell Me a Story (2018).

Personal Life and Relationships

Moreau and Christopher have one child together, a son named Casper Hayes Redman who was born on July 16, 2015.
