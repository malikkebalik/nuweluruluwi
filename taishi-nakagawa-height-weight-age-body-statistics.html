Taishi Nakagawa is a Japanese actor, host, voice actor, and model. He started his career as a child actor and has been cast in many projects including Closest Love to Heaven, Team Batista 2: General Rouge no Gaisen, I’m Mita, Your Housekeeper, Boys Over Flowers Season 2, Blue Demon ver.2.0, Ohisama, When I was a Child, Taira no Kiyomori, GTO Aki mo Oni Abar Supesharu!, 13-sai no Hello Work, ReLIFE, Perfect Blue, Yakou Kanransha, My Blood & Bones in a Flowing Galaxy, Water Polo Yankees, Konya wa Kokoro Dake Daite, Prison School, Minami kun no Koibito – My Little Lover, Kakegurui – Compulsive Gambler, Sleepeeer Hit!, Kakegurui Season 2, Are You Ready? Hey You Girl!, Boku no Satsui ga Koi o Shita, Chibi Maruko-chan: A Boy From Italy, Rising Wakaoka Genius Awakening, Hidarite Ippon no Shuuto, Kids on the Slope, Hanjirou, Tsuugaku Series Tsuugaku Densha, Tsuugaku Series Tsuugaku Tochu, Unrequited Love, Rainbow Days, Josee, the Tiger and the Fish, Inubu: The Dog Club, and Samurai Marathon.

Taishi Nakagawa Facts

  • In 2011, he played the character of Kakeru Asuda, the oldest son in the Asuda family who is a hot-headed person and always makes rash decisions but is very protective of his family members, in the family mystery drama series I am Mita, Your Housekeeper.
  • Taishi Nakagawa has been friends with actors Ryo Yoshizawa, Yudai Chiba, and Ryunosuke Kamiki.
  • Alongside Hana Sugisaki, Tatsuomi Hamada, and Sho Hirano, Taishi Nakagawa co-starred as Tenma Hase in the 2nd season of the teen romantic drama series Boys Over Flowers in 2018.
  • He was cast as Fujii in the 2019 Japanese-British historical action-adventure film Samurai Marathon, based on the 2014 novel The Marathon Samurai: Five Tales of Japan’s First Marathon by Akihiro Dobashi.
  • For his work in both Kids on the Slope and Lock-On Love, he won the 42nd Japan Academy Prize in the “Newcomer of the Year” category in 2019.
  • Taishi Nakagawa has appeared in the music videos of songs like Pinky Santa by Boyfriend, Together and Itsuka Kitto by Naoto Inti Raymi, Shoudou by Haku, and Message by Sonoko Inoue.
  • Some of his hobbies include fishing and playing guitar, basketball, and football.
  • Taishi Nakagawa provided vocals to the character of Tsuneo Suzukawa in the 2020 animated romantic comedy-drama film Josee, the Tiger and the Fish, based on the short story and young adult novel of the same name by Seiko Tanabe.
