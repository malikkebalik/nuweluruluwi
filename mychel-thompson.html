Biography

Mychel Thompson was born in Los Angeles, CA on June 1, 1988. Basketball player who played at Pepperdine University from 2007 to 2011, becoming one of the best players in school history during that time. He made his NBA debut with the Cleveland Cavaliers in 2012 before playing with D-league teams like the Erie BayHawks and Santa Cruz Warriors. During his time with the Santa Cruz Warriors, he played with Seth Curry while both of their brothers were playing in the back court of the Golden State Warriors. He played high school basketball at Jesuit High School in Portland, Oregon for two years before transferring to Santa Margarita Catholic High School in Rancho Santa Margarita, California.
