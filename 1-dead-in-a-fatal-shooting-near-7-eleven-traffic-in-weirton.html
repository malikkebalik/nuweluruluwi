1 dead in a fatal shooting near 7 Eleven traffic in Weirton

The Weirton Police Department and Weirton SWAT responded to an active barricade situation near the 7-Eleven traffic area on Friday, escalating into an intense standoff. Law enforcement officials were alerted to an active shooter at a nearby residence. Upon arrival, they located a male suspect who retreated into the home and self-barricaded.

Multiple law enforcement vehicles, including SWAT units, converged at the scene, surrounding the house where the suspect was barricaded. This incident unfolded at a Pennsylvania Ave residence, causing traffic redirection in the area.

According to reports, Carl Gist, 82, identified as the suspect in the shooting, has died. The victim, Cassandra Jeter, 67, sustained injuries and was airlifted to a Pittsburgh hospital for treatment; her current condition remains undisclosed.

The West Virginia State Police have initiated an investigation into the shooting incident, which transpired along Weir Avenue. Both the suspect and the victim received medical attention following the confrontation.

After hours of negotiation attempts, law enforcement deployed tear gas into the residence in an effort to prompt the suspect’s surrender. Subsequently, when the individual emerged armed, law enforcement engaged and ultimately shot the suspect.
