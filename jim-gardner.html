Biography

Jim Gardner was born in New York City, NY on May 17, 1948. News anchor known for his work with WPVI-TV, a Philadelphia, Pennsylvania ABC affiliate. Earlier in his career, he worked for a New York City CBS Radio station. He guest-starred (as himself) in an episode of Philly, a series that starred actress Kim Delaney. In 1970, he graduated from Columbia University with a degree in political science.
