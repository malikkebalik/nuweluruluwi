Who is Catherine Disher?

Catherine Disher is a Canadian actress and comedian best known for her role in the TV series, ‘Good Witch’ and ‘Snakes and Ladders’. Disher won two Gemini Awards in 2005 and 2010 for Best Actress for her role in ‘Snakes and Ladders and ‘The Border’.

Catherine Disher: Movies, TV Series

Catherine Disher made her acting debut in 1986 with the role of Catherine Collins in the movie, ‘The Vindicator’. The same year, she was also featured in the TV film, ‘Murder Sees the Light’. During the 90s, she featured in the TV films, ‘Earthquake in New York and ‘Woman on the Run: The Lawrencia Bembenek Story’,

She did several television movies during the 200s. She landed her best-known and breakthrough role as Martha Tinsdale in ‘The Good Witch’. The film, which starred Chris Potter and Catherine Bell has ten sequels. In 2015, she portrayed Kate in ‘Regression.’ It is a psychological thriller mystery. It is an investigation on the case of a man who admits to having sexually abused his teenage daughter but has no recollection.

On TV, she has made appearances in ‘Forever Knight’ as Dr. Natalie Lambert and in ‘Snakes and Ladders as Minister Audrey Flankman. In 2008, she featured in the drama ‘The Border’. Furthermore, some of her other acting credits include ‘Goosebumps’, ‘Degrassi’, ‘Street Legal’, ‘Night Heat’, ‘Kung Fu: The Legend Continues, ‘The Campbells’, and ‘The Path to 9/11’.

Disher won two Gemini Awards in 2005 and 2010 for Best Actress for her role in ‘Snakes and Ladders and ‘The Border’.
