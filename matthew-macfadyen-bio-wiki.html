Who is Matthew Macfadyen?

Matthew Macfadyen is an English actor, theatre artist, and voice actor. He is famous for his role in “Pride and Prejudice,” “Death at a Funeral,” “Spooks,” and “The Nutcracker and the For Realms.”

Matthew Macfadyen: Bio, Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Education

Matthew Macfadyen was born on 17th October 1974 in Great Yarmouth, Norfolk, England. As of 2023, his age is 48 years old.

Moreover, his ethnicity is mixed and according to the astrological chart, his birth sign is Libra. He holds British nationality.

His father’s name is Martin Macfadyen who was a businessman. His mother’s name is Meinir Owen, by profession she was a Teacher. He grew up with his sibling named Jamie Macfadyen.

He studied schools based in England, Scotland, and Indonesia and finally completed his school in Rutland. Then he joined the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art and completed his studies.
