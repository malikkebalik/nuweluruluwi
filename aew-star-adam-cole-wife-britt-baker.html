Early Life & Education

Brittany Baker is a versatile individual who has excelled not only in professional wrestling but also in the field of dentistry.

She was born on April 23, 1991, in Punxsutawney, Pennsylvania, to her parents, Sam Baker and Mary Ann Baker.

Britt grew up with her younger brother Dane.

She later attended Pennsylvania State University, where she studied behavioral medicine with a minor in human development and family studies.

She began her professional wrestling training by enrolling in the International Wrestling Cartel training academy in South Hills, Pennsylvania, in 2014.

There, she trained under the guidance of Super Hentai and Marshall Gambino.

In addition to her wrestling training, she also pursued a career in dentistry, enrolling in the University Of Pittsburgh School Of Dental Medicine in the same year.

Despite time management challenges that came with pursuing two different sectors, Baker managed to balance both and graduated from the School of Dental Medicine in May 2018.

During her time studying dentistry, she also trained in Absolute Intense Wrestling in Cleveland, Ohio, under the mentorship of Johnny Gargano and Candice LeRae.

Wrestling Career

Britt began her professional wrestling career in 2015 when she debuted at an International Wrestling Cartel (IWC) event. 

In 2016, she appeared on WWE Raw as a jobber but unfortunately lost to Nia Jax in a squash match.

Nevertheless, her career took a significant turn when she signed with All Elite Wrestling (AEW), a newly formed wrestling promotion, in January 2019.

With this, she became the first contracted female wrestler in the company. Also, this signing marked a significant milestone in her professional career.

She debuted for AEW on May 25, 2019, at the promotion’s inaugural pay-per-view event, Double or Nothing.

In an impressive performance, she emerged victorious in a four-way match against Nyla Rose, Kylie Rae, and Awesome Kong.

This victory catapulted her to the forefront of AEW’s women’s division, and she has since become a prominent figure in the company.

How Did Adam And Britt Meet?

The couple reportedly met on the popular dating app, Bumble during their early days in the wrestling industry.

Regardless of their busy schedule, they managed to flourish their romance and soon started dating.

Fast forward to 2021, and the couple finally got to work together professionally for the first time when Cole switched from WWE to AEW.

It seems that their love and passion for wrestling brought them together, as they now share both a personal and professional life.

According to various sources, the couple currently resides together in Sunny, Florida, where they continue to pursue their dreams and support each other every step of the way.

A Brief On Adam Cole

Austin Kirk Jenkins, better known by his ring name Adam Cole, is an accomplished American professional wrestler.

Currently signed with All Elite Wrestling (AEW), Cole has also made a name for himself with previous stints in WWE and Ring of Honor (ROH).

Cole’s impressive career began in 2009 when he joined ROH, where he became the promotions first-ever three-time World Champion.

He also won the 2014 Survival of the Fittest Tournament and held the ROH World Television Championship.

Cole has wrestled for various independent promotions, including Chikara and Combat Zone Wrestling (CZW), where he held the CZW World Junior Heavyweight Championship.

He also enjoyed successful Pro Wrestling Guerrilla (PWG) runs as the longest-reigning PWG World Champion.

In addition to his work in the United States, Cole has also wrestled for New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW) as a member of the infamous Bullet Club stable.

Is Britt Baker Also A Dentist?

Britt Baker is a talented individual who not only excels in professional wrestling but also practices dentistry. She is a proud member of the American Dental Association and is currently employed at a private practice in Winter Park, Florida.

In a 2018 interview, Baker revealed that dentistry was her “forever dream job” and that she had no intention of giving it up.

Despite her success in wrestling, she remains committed to practicing dentistry and intends to continue doing so both during and after her wrestling career.

Baker has also shown impressive management skills, as she has arranged a special agreement with her employer to remain closed on Wednesdays.

This arrangement allows her to travel and perform on AEW Dynamite without interfering with her dental practice schedules.
