Glenbrook Mall Fatal Shooting: Active Shooter, One shot at Glenbrook Square Mall in Fort Wayne, Indiana

Glenbrook Mall Shooting: Authorities are investigating after one person was shot at Glenbrook Square Mall in Fort Wayne, Indiana. The incident occurred on Friday, September 1, 2023, at Glenbrook Square Mall, located at 4201 Coldwater Rd., Fort Wayne, IN. Multiple law enforcement officers responded to the area for a shooting report.

There is no word yet on a suspect. The public is advised to use caution in the area.

This is a breaking news story. An update will be posted as more information becomes available.

What Happened at Glenbrook Square Mall in Fort Wayne, Indiana

Police confirm they are responding to a shooting at Glenbrook Square Mall Friday afternoon that left one person injured.

According to police, there was one shooting near the JC Penny fountain. The officers have not confirmed their condition.

Everyone is asked to avoid the area surrounding the mall.

On July 8, one person was shot in the upper leg during what police called an argument inside the mall’s H&M store. Police later arrested and charged Gregory Normil with the shooting.
