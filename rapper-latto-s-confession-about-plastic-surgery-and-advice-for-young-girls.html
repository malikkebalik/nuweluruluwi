Rapper Latto's Confession About Plastic Surgery and Advice for Young Girls


Rapper Latto has revealed that she underwent plastic surgery.

During her ‘777 Radio’ show on Apple Music, she discussed the challenges of undergoing cosmetic procedures and highlighted that people often assume her body is natural. 

Latto advised young girls considering “minor adjustments” to develop a healthy appreciation for their bodies, cautioning that they may become fixated on repeated surgical procedures and struggle to meet everyone’s expectations.

She went on to disclose that despite her surgery, she still edits her photos and experiences wardrobe mishaps. The rapper emphasized that growing up in the public eye from the age of 16 influenced her decision to undergo the surgery.
